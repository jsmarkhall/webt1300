What Do I Do?

Today I make music, design artwork for musical works, occasionally play video games and generally I just like to do a lot of "nerdy stuff."

Creating Music

For my music creation I use the DAW FL Studio 20. There's different hardware and instruments I use as well such as a a Fender Squier Jazzmaster, an LTD EC-256 with some seymour duncan pickups installed, and I record those with a Focusrite Scarlett Solo 3rd Generation bridged by a connection through a BOSS GT-6 Guitar Effects Processor. There's also the one thing that makes it all happen: My computer. Built with an Intel(R) Core(TM) i7-8700K CPU @ 3.70GHz, Nvidia Geforce GTX 1060 6GB it gets the job done for sure.

Creating Art

For this section I'll mainly let the art speak for itself. These are from various different albums I've worked on. I use GIMP for image manipulation and sources for these images can come from a variety of different areas. Sometimes they're just based on photographs, sometimes it's all in the program itself. Or sometimes there's other weird sources.

Video Games?

I do enjoy playing some video games from time to time. As of 9/30/21 I am waiting for the arrival of Halo: Infinite, Alan Wake Remastered and what the future holds for Cyberpunk 2077.

What Makes Wahoo Nerdy?

Well as you saw by my computer setup I love color coordination with everything. I configure allpapers within a program called Wallpaper Engine to match all of my RGB lighting. I love to do ReShading for video games, I enjoy working with synthesizers as a music producer and really I love the world of electronics. Sometimes I'll spend hours just learning how to do something random on a computer. For example, I've given my Amazon Echo the ability to open Playnite, a universal game launcher that I've fully configured to make my own. Within that I can launch a portable version of Kodi running off of an external hard drive, and pretty much everything is controlled with my gamepad for the closest thing to a console experience without actually using a console.
